- Open the log window to see each tracker actual firmware version <br /> 
(the log window is located under "Connect & Power Off" in the software).


- The trackers are updated wirelessly 📶 — no USB cable is needed.<br /> 
🚫 Do not update the tracker and receiver at the same time.<br /> 


- If the update fails, need restart the tracker and click update again.<br /> 
&emsp;&emsp;🟩Green – fast blink: Tracker working normally<br /> 
&emsp;&emsp;🟩Green – slow blink: Tracker waiting for receiver signal<br /> 
&emsp;&emsp;🟦Blue: Tracker is receiving firmware data<br /> 
&emsp;&emsp;🟨Yellow: Update failed (manually press the 🔘 button to restart, then retry the update)<br /> 
&emsp;&emsp;⬜White: Update successful (usually auto‑restarts after 10s; if not, restart manually)<br />

<div style="display: flex; flex-direction: row; gap: 30px; flex-wrap: wrap; align-items: flex-start; margin: 25px 0;">
<div style="flex: 1; min-width: 250px; text-align: center;">


![A_pose-6]( ../../static/img/unboxing/A_pose-6.jpg )


</div>
<div style="flex: 1.5; min-width: 300px" class="tutorial-step-text">
<strong style="font-size: 1.15em" class="tutorial-step-title">A pose</strong><br />
- Raise VR controller to avoid tracker capturing magnets in controller.<br />
- Keep proper foot spacing, neither too close nor too wide, similar to the image. <br />
- Stand naturally and relaxed without tensing your muscles.
</div>
</div>
<!-- ==================== 步骤 1 结束 ==================== -->

<!-- ==================== 步骤 2：S pose 开始 ==================== -->
<div style="display: flex; flex-direction: row; gap: 30px; flex-wrap: wrap; align-items: flex-start; margin: 25px 0;">
<div style="flex: 1; min-width: 250px; text-align: center;">


![S_pose-6]( ../../static/img/unboxing/S_pose-6.jpg )


</div>
<div style="flex: 1.5; min-width: 300px" class="tutorial-step-text">
<strong style="font-size: 1.15em" class="tutorial-step-title">S pose</strong><br />
- Half squat + bend waist and lower head, so that the tracker can determine the forward direction of the body through the tilt angle. <br />
- If bending is difficult or unclear, you can use Advanced Calibration.<br />
- Keep legs balanced + knee spacing.
</div>
</div>
<!-- ==================== 步骤 2 结束 ==================== -->

<!-- ==================== 步骤 3：B pose 开始 ==================== -->
<div style="display: flex; flex-direction: row; gap: 30px; flex-wrap: wrap; align-items: flex-start; margin: 25px 0;">
<div style="flex: 1; min-width: 250px; text-align: center;">


![B_pose-6]( ../../static/img/unboxing/B_pose-6.jpg )


</div>
<div style="flex: 1.5; min-width: 300px" class="tutorial-step-text">
<strong style="font-size: 1.15em" class="tutorial-step-title">B pose (Advanced Calibration)</strong><br />
- Separate S pose bending into bowing posture for recording.<br />
- This avoids body distortion caused by S pose<br />
 (commonly seen as the skeleton system tilting to the side after sitting down).
</div>
</div>
